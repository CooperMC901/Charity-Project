What we do

At Cooper's dog charity, we provide dogs the long, happy, and healthy lives they deserve to live. We supply housing, food, and shelter to dogs only and proudly are located in Tupelo, MS. Thanks to the generous donations of our community, we continue to operate as a non-profit and plan to operate this way for decades to come.

How can I help?

Cooper's Dog Charity is currently accepting applications for volunteers throughout the 2022 winter. These volunteers will be expected to involve themselves with the community to rescue animals out of the cold, harsh conditions. As well as volunteering, visit our "Donate!" webpage to make a one-time or monthly contribution to our mission. We thank you in advance for your support!

Impact

Over the past decade, Cooper's Dog Charity has rescued nearly 5,000 animals to date and placed nearly 4,300 in forever homes. These dogs were rescued from all types of conditions including abuse, neglect, and harsh environmental conditions. We want all dogs to be loved and won't stop until no dog has to suffer in our community!
